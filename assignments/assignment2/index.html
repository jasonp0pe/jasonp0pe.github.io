About Dolphins

Dolphins are great.

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and social nature. They communicate with each other using a complex system of clicks, whistles, and body language. Dolphins are also known for their acrobatic abilities, often seen leaping out of the water and performing flips.
